Clinical Case Presentation

Analytical Reasoning • Evidence-Based Discussion • Clinical Excellence

Eligibility

  • 1st Year MBBS to CRMI
  • Individual participation only

Abstract Submission

  • PDF format (≤ 5 MB)
  • Through designated Google Form
  • File name: Name_College_Year
  • Last date: May 4,2026

Abstract Format

  • History
  • Examination Findings
  • Provisional / Differential Diagnosis
  • Investigations
  • Management

Specifications

  • Word limit: 500 words
  • Font: Times New Roman
  • Font size: 11
  • Line spacing: 1.0
  • Images of relevant investigation may be included
  • Certification letter from the Head of Department is mandatory

Selection Process

  • All submitted abstracts will be reviewed by an esteemed panel of faculty.
  • Top abstracts will be shortlisted for the final presentation round.
  • Results will be announced by May 10, 2026.
  • Shortlisted finalists will be intimated individually.

Presentation

  • 6 June 2026
  • 10:30 AM onwards
  • 5 min presentation + 2 min discussion
  • Email slides before event
  • Finalists are also advised to bring their hardcopy of the Powerpoint
  • Judges’ decision final

Registration fee

  • Initial Registration: ₹200
  • Selected candidates need to pay an extra ₹100 for final presentation
