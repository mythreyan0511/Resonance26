About Government Vellore Medical College and Hospital

Government Vellore Medical College and Hospital, located in Adukkamparai, Tamil Nadu, is one of the state's premier medical institutions. Established with the mission to provide excellent medical education and healthcare, GVMC has consistently produced skilled and compassionate doctors. With its dedicated faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and a strong emphasis on community service, GVMC stands as a beacon of medical excellence in the region.

Foreword

Resonance ’26 represents a confluence of intellect, inquiry, and academic aspiration. This conclave seeks to move beyond conventional learning by encouraging participants to analyze, question, and reason clinically. Through quizzes, case presentations, model exhibitions, and creative academic engagement, Resonance aims to cultivate scientific thinking and disciplined competition. We welcome all participants to resonate with reason, precision, and excellence.

General Instructions

Kindly follow these guidelines for a smooth experience

  • Delegates must complete registration before 8:30 AM.
  • Participants are required to assemble at least 30 minutes prior to their respective events.
  • A valid college ID card is mandatory for all participants.
  • Complimentary lunch will be provided for all registered delegates.
  • As part of our sustainability initiative, delegates are requested to carry their own water bottles, as single-use plastics will not be provided.
  • Participants must strictly adhere to the event schedule.
  • Professional conduct and discipline must be maintained at all times within the venue.
  • Any form of malpractice will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Mobile phones must be kept on silent mode inside the venue.
  • Participants are responsible for their personal belongings.
  • The decision of the organizing committee shall be final and binding.
